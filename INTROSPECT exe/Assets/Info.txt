
Instructions  /  体验指示:

  -  Please face directly towards your device camera. / 请面向设备摄像头。

  -  First click on the Open-Eye icon at bottom center to check camera preview. Adjust your position if necessary, for the program to effectively track your eyes.
     /需先点击中间的睁眼图标以查看程序所读取的摄像头画面。为了程序能更准确的捕捉、识别双眼，请根据情况调整自己或设备的位置。

  -  EAR : Eye Aspect Ratio on the bottom left is adjustable, controls the threshold for the computer to determine open/closed eyes. (Average between 0.4 ~ 0.5) 
     /左下角的EAR阈值可直接输入调改，其控制计算机视觉对于用户睁眼或闭眼的判断。阈值越高，灯光越容易被触发； 反之越低，光越容易熄灭。

  -  Once ready, close camera preview by clicking the same icon, and maximize the program window. Initiate the experience simply by closing your eyes. 
     /准备好后，再点击睁眼图标以关闭摄像头预览，放大程序窗口，闭上双眼即可开启体验。

  *  DO NOT proceed if there are any discomforts or concerns associating with eyes and vision.
  *  如有任何双眼、视觉相关的不适或忧虑，请勿继续体验。

About Human Vision  /  关于人类视觉:


A range between 380 ~ 760 nanometers of the electromagnetic wavelength is the perceptible scope of light entering the human cornea, which are converted into 
neuronal signals by the retina, to be processed by the visual cortex of our brain. Constructing what and how we see.
当适宜波长范围内（380 ~ 760纳米）的电磁波刺激，经折光系统与视网膜，作为视神经信号由大脑进行编码、分析后，便产生视觉。

Our visual perception is responsible for at least 80% of the information we acquire, and 80% of our memories.
我们的视觉感知负责至少80%以上外界信息的接收，与我们80%的记忆。

With just an average weight of 28 grams, human eyes are constituted by over 2 million working parts, hundreds of millions of photoreceptor cells and billions of neurons.
人眼区区28克的重量，却具备两百多万个组成部分，一亿多个感光细胞与近百亿个神经元。

As the most active muscle group, our eyes can process 36,000 pieces of information in a single hour, it is considered as the second most complex organ after the brain.
作为人体最活跃的肌群，我们的眼睛每小时能处理36,000个信息，是除去大脑，人体最复杂的器官。
